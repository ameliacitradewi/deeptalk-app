Privacy Policy

Last updated: July, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Deep Talk app (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles your information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect
Deep Talk does not collect, store, or share any personal data.
* No account creation required
* No email, name, or phone number collected
* No tracking of conversation responses or behavior
* No integration with social media or contacts

2. Local Data Storage
All app content (e.g., selected categories, question progress) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

3. Analytics & Crash Reports
We may use anonymous analytics tools (e.g., Apple App Analytics, Google Play Console) to understand app performance. These tools do not identify users personally.

4. Third-Party Services
Deep Talk does not use third-party services that collect personal information.

5. Children’s Privacy
This app is safe for all users and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend reviewing this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
