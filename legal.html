Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2025

1. No Personal Data Collected

Deep Talk does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

  • • No account registration is required.
  • • No names, emails, or personal identifiers are collected.
  • • No location tracking or analytics are used.
  • • No cookies or trackers are used.

2. No Third-Party Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we do not share any information with third parties.


3. No In-App Purchases

The app does not contain in-app purchases or require any payment information.


4. Children's Privacy

Deep Talk is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 17. If we become aware that such data has been collected, we will promptly delete it.


5. Changes to This Policy

If we update our privacy practices in the future, we will update this policy and notify users accordingly.
